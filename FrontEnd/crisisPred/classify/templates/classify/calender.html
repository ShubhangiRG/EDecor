Disposition of the Crisis Template (one template per person per crisis involved contact) as reported by the officer. This graph is the frequency representation for the Disposition column

This graph shows the approximate size of total number of unique initial call types and their relative frequencies. We see that the class frequencies fall within a range and aren't separated by large values.

The frequency of how officers are distributed across various bureaus and racial distribution within each bureau

We see that maximum officers are White. The pie chart instantaneously brings out the racial imbalance in public service positions.

The bar graph easily shows the approximate differnece in numbers of the one majority vs all other minorities.

Graph depicts that some records contain improperly filled values. Years cannot be in negative

Since the count of these records is too small, relative to the size of dataset, we remove these from the dataset. Also some classes don't make sense, like the "Unknown" class. So we eliminate those records too.

The obtained scatter plot is a better depiction of the years of experience of officers wrt their race.
